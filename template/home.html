Brief Overview

Our AI will write a story for you. It was trained on the text from millions of web pages, using machine learning to figure out what to write. Each story it creates is unique and not something that ever existed before.

The AI model takes in the data uploaded by the user and gets trained on the same. The training time could be more or less depending upon the number of epochs provided by the user.

This app challenges you to give these documents a machine learning makeover. Improving the ease of data enhancement will mesmerize you.

The AI used sequential model Long Short Term Memory algorithm for training purpose, and along with various tools like Pandas, Numpy, Keras etc. goes on to generate real like story.

Applications of the App :

This app could be used to generate new type of story or poem never existed before.
